Sfira SMS Reminder Terms of Service

1. Program Description
The Sfira SMS Reminder program sends one daily SMS reminder during the 49-day Omer counting period to help you remember to count the Omer.

2. Opt-Out Instructions
You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Just text "STOP" to our number. After sending "STOP", we will confirm your unsubscription and you will no longer receive SMS messages from us. If you wish to join again, just sign up through our website and we will resume sending messages.

4. Carrier Liability

5. Message and Data Rates
Message and data rates may apply. You will receive one message per day during the Omer period. Please check with your mobile provider for details about your plan.
