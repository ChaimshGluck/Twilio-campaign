Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you sign up for the Sfira SMS Reminder program.

Information We Collect

We collect your phone number solely for the purpose of sending you daily SMS reminders. We do not collect any additional personal data.

How We Use Your Information

Your phone number is only used to send you the daily SMS messages you signed up for. We do not use your information for marketing purposes or share it with third-party advertisers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your information with third parties. We may share data with service providers strictly for the purpose of delivering the SMS messages you signed up to receive.

Your Consent

By signing up, you consent to receiving one SMS per day during the Omer counting period. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.
